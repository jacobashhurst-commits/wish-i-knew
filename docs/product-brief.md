# Product Brief

Wish I Knew helps Australian expecting and new parents know what is coming next.

The emotional problem is: **What am I forgetting?**

The product answers that with a personalised parenting timeline, a weekly lookahead ritual and playful illustrated milestone cards.

## MVP Audience

- Australian first-time parents
- expecting parents
- parents with babies aged 0-24 months
- partners sharing the mental load
- parents planning childcare, return to work, solids, immunisations and practical gear shifts

## MVP Promise

Within 10 seconds, a tired parent should understand the next useful thing worth knowing.

## Initial Screens

- Landing page
- Onboarding
- Home
- Timeline
- Card detail
- Saved cards
- Admin cards
- Settings

Phase 1 only creates the foundation. It does not build the full screen set.
